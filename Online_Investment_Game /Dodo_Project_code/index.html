Let's Play Around!

There is one fund acts better than the others. Can you figure out which one is it?

Among these 25 funds, there is one fund that has a annual growing rate of 9% on average,
and the rest funds all have 5% as the annual growing rate on average.
If you can figure that fund out, you are more likely to beat the market.
The blanks are for you to manage the percentages of how would you like to buy the funds,
so the sum cannot exceed 100(%), and if it does, a warning will show up.
The previous 3 year performance is already given.
You can use all the precede growing rates of each funds to guess which one has 9% as the average
annually growing rate.

Note: it is highly recommend to have a sum of your input numbers as 100 in order to be more
likely to beat the market, since the market tends to grow.

The game will end at the 15th year. At that time, if your cumulative profit is larger than
the cumulative profit of the market, then you beat the market! Congratulations!!
